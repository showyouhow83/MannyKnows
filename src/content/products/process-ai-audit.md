Most small businesses don't need more software — they need someone to look at how the work actually flows and fix the parts that leak time. That's what this is.

## I map how you really run

I sit with you and trace the day-to-day: where leads come in, how they're handled, what gets re-typed by hand, where things stall or slip through. Twenty years of operations work means I know where to look.

## I find the highest-leverage fixes

You get a short, prioritized plan — quick wins first, bigger builds flagged for later. Where AI genuinely helps, I'll say so; where it's just hype, I'll say that too. Wherever a decision was missing the data to make it, I fill that gap.

## Then I help you do it

This isn't a report that sits in a drawer. I stay on to build the automations, wire up the tools, and ship the fixes — with you in the loop the whole way.
