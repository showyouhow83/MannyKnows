Search has two front doors now: Google, and the AI assistants people increasingly ask instead. We get you found at both — then convert the visits you already get. Traffic you can't measure and a site people bounce off are both fixable, and we work every end.

## Get found — on Google *and* in AI answers

Technical SEO, clean structured data (JSON-LD), fast pages, and the on-page fundamentals search engines actually reward. No tricks, no filler. The same foundations make you legible to the crawlers *and* to the AI engines reading the web.

## Show up when people ask an AI

ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google's AI Overviews answer questions by pulling from sources they can read and trust — and for a lot of buyers, that's the new first search. This is **AEO / GEO** (answer-engine and generative-engine optimization), and it rewards the same things good SEO does, sharpened for machines: clear, well-structured content; rich schema and entity signals; direct answers to the questions your customers actually ask; and a site that's fast and crawlable. We build your pages so an AI can understand what you do, who you serve, and why to recommend you — making you *eligible to be cited*, not just ranked.

## Read the traffic, cut the friction

We run Lighthouse and analytics to see where visitors really drop off, then remove the friction step by step. It's the same loop we ran on enterprise eCommerce — A/B tests and behavior data that, on one store, lifted traffic 145% and sales 65%.

## Measure what matters

Clear dashboards and reporting — including how you're showing up in search and, increasingly, in AI answers — so you can see what's working instead of guessing.
